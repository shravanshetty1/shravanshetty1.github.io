Recent Works
Blawgd

Blawgd

Censorship resistant blogging platform. It achieves censorship resistance and decentralization from blockchain technology. A new post, like, follow .etc. are all stored on the blockchain. The light clients (website and apps) verify all data received from nodes of the blockchain network using merkle proofs, making it impossible for any man in the middle to censor information without being detected. (Currently unimplemented) - All nodes on the network communicate over TOR making the network highly resistant to takedowns from external entities.
